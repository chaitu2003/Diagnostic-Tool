The Basic Diagnostic Tool is a self-assesment utility designed to provide insights based on your selected symptoms. By analysing common symptom patterns and matching them with known medical conditions, it helps you to identify potential health issue for further exploration.

This tool Does NOT replace professional medical advice but helps as a first step in understanding what might affecting your health. Please do contact a professional for accurate diagnosis.

How it works:
  • Select your Symptom/Symptoms from the list.
  • click the Submit button.
  • In the result section you will find the matched disease/diseases for your symptom/symptoms with confidence percentages.
